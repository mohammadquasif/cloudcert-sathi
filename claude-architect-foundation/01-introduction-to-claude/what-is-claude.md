## 📖 What Is This Topic About?

Claude is an AI assistant created by **Anthropic**. It is designed to be helpful, harmless, and honest.

Unlike many AI tools, Claude is built with **safety as a core feature** — not an afterthought.

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts

- **Claude** — An AI assistant by Anthropic, built for natural language understanding and generation
- **Anthropic** — The AI safety company that created Claude
- **Constitutional AI** — The method Anthropic uses to train Claude to be safe and aligned with human values
- **Helpful, Harmless, Honest (3H)** — The three core principles Claude follows

---

## 📘 Claude's Core Principles

### Helpful
Claude is designed to be genuinely useful. It tries to understand what the user actually needs — not just what they literally asked.

### Harmless
Claude is trained to avoid generating content that could cause harm — whether physical, emotional, social, or otherwise.

### Honest
Claude tries to be truthful. It will tell you when it doesn't know something. It won't pretend to be certain when it isn't.

---

## 🇮🇳 Indian Real-Life Example

**Think of Claude like a very knowledgeable professor:**

Imagine a professor who:
- Always tries to give you the most useful answer
- Refuses to help you cheat on an exam (harmless)
- Honestly tells you "I am not sure about this, please verify" instead of guessing

That is the spirit of how Claude is designed to behave.

---

## 🔑 Exam-Focused Points

- ✅ Claude is created by **Anthropic** (not Google, not OpenAI)
- ✅ The 3 core principles are: **Helpful, Harmless, Honest**
- ✅ **Constitutional AI** is the key training approach Anthropic uses
- ✅ Claude is designed with **safety-first** philosophy
- ✅ Claude supports very long **context windows** — meaning it can process long documents in one go

## 🧩 Scenario-Based Thinking

**Scenario:** A company wants to deploy an AI assistant that users can use to get product support. They are concerned about the AI giving wrong information confidently.

**Which Claude principle addresses this concern most directly?**

**Answer:** **Honesty** — Claude is trained to express uncertainty rather than state wrong information with false confidence.

## ❓ Chapter Practice Questions

**Q1.** Which company created Claude?

- A) OpenAI
- B) Google DeepMind
- C) Anthropic
- D) Amazon

**Answer:** C

**Explanation:** Claude is created by Anthropic, an AI safety company. It is not affiliated with OpenAI (which makes ChatGPT), Google DeepMind, or Amazon, though Claude is available through Amazon Bedrock.

---

**Q2.** What are the three core principles that guide Claude's design?

- A) Fast, Scalable, Secure
- B) Helpful, Harmless, Honest
- C) Intelligent, Responsive, Efficient
- D) Creative, Accurate, Consistent

**Answer:** B

**Explanation:** The three H's — Helpful, Harmless, Honest — are the foundational principles behind Claude's design and training approach.
