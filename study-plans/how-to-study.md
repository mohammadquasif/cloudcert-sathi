# How to Study Using This Repo

Welcome, student! Before you dive into the notes and questions, take 5 minutes to read this file.

It will save you a lot of confusion later and help you use this repo in the most effective way.

---

| Exam | Folder to Go To |

| AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner | `aws-cloud-practitioner/` |
| Claude Architect Foundation | `claude-architect-foundation/` |

---

## The 4-Phase Study Method

This repo follows a simple 4-phase method. Each phase builds on the previous one.

```
Phase 1 → Understand
Phase 2 → Practice
Phase 3 → Revise
Phase 4 → Test Yourself
```

---

## Phase 1 — Understand (Week 1 to Week 3)

Read the chapter notes one chapter at a time.

**How to read a chapter:**

1. Read the full chapter notes once (don't try to memorize)
2. Re-read the "Exam-Focused Points" section carefully
3. Read the Indian real-life example — it helps the concept stick
4. Try to explain the concept to yourself in your own words

**Do not skip chapters.** The concepts build on each other.

---

## Phase 2 — Practice (After Each Chapter)

After finishing a chapter, immediately attempt the practice questions for that chapter.

**Important:**
- Try to answer first without looking at the explanation
- If you get it wrong, read the full explanation carefully
- Write down why you got it wrong — this is very important!

Keep a notebook (or a simple text file) where you note:
- Topics you found confusing
- Questions you got wrong and why

---

## Phase 3 — Revise (Week 4 / Penultimate Week)

Use the flashcards in the `flashcards/` folder for quick revision.

**How to use flashcards:**
- Go through 20–30 flashcards every day
- Cover the answer and try to recall it
- Put cards you struggle with in a "struggle pile" and revisit them

Also re-read the "Quick Revision Summary" section at the bottom of each chapter file.

---

## Phase 4 — Test Yourself (Last Week)

Take the mock tests from the `mock-tests/` folder.

**Rules for mock tests:**
- Set a timer (65 minutes for a 65-question mock)
- Do not look at notes during the test
- Review every question after — especially the ones you got right by guessing

**Target scores:**
- If you are scoring 70%+ consistently → You are exam-ready
- If you are scoring below 70% → Go back to the weak chapters

## Daily Study Habit

**The 3-Block Method:**

```
Block 1 (20 min) — Revise yesterday's topic briefly
Block 2 (30 min) — Study today's new chapter
Block 3 (10 min) — Attempt 5-10 practice questions
```

Total: ~60 minutes per day. Consistent 60 minutes beats random 3-hour sessions.

---

## What NOT To Do

- ❌ Do not try to memorize every service name from day one
- ❌ Do not skip practice questions
- ❌ Do not start mock tests before finishing all chapters
- ❌ Do not look at exam dumps — it wastes your time and won't help you truly understand
- ❌ Do not study for 5 hours one day and then skip 3 days

---

## A Word of Encouragement

These exams are **beginner-friendly**. AWS Cloud Practitioner is designed for people who are just starting with cloud. Claude Architect Foundation is designed for people exploring AI responsibly.

You don't need to be a developer. You don't need prior experience.

You just need:
- Consistency (study every day)
- Curiosity (ask "why?" when something is confusing)
- Patience (it will click eventually)

**Keep going. You will get there. 🌟**
